AI is reshaping enterprise purchasing as escalating infrastructure costs push companies from assembled stacks of specialized tools toward consolidated platforms, creating significant expansion opportunities for Elastic partners. Elasticsearch sits at the intersection of AI-powered search, unified observability, and modern security operations on a single data layer, enabling natural platform expansion within customer accounts with compounding service opportunities that point solutions cannot match. Jesse Sladek, expanding his role to oversee Global Partner Sales, Strategy, Enablement, Sales Programs, and Renewals, argues that partners who move early on this consolidation trend will win the platform cycle.
